Available Vaccines

Hepatitis B Vaccine

Hepatitis B is a viral disease that causes liver inflammation and damage. Over time, chronic hepatitis B infection can cause cirrhosis, liver failure, and liver cancer.

Read more

Tdap vaccine

The Tdap vaccine is a combination vaccine used to boost immunity against tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis. There is also the Td vaccine that protects against tetanus and diphtheria.

Read more

Hib vaccines

protects against Haemophilus influenzae type b, a bacteria that can cause pneumonia, meningitis (inflammation of the protective membrane surrounding the brain )

Read more

Pneumococcal Vaccine

protect against Streptococcus pneumoniae, a bacteria that can cause pneumonia, meningitis, and bacteremia (bacterial infection of the bloodstream)

Read more

MMR vaccine

The MMR(Measles, Mumps, Rubella)vaccine is a combination vaccine that protects against different diseases: Measles , Mumbes , Rubella.

Read more

Rotavirus Vaccine

Rotavirus is a viral infection that can cause severe gastroenteritis in infants and children, leading the dehydration and, in some cases, death.
